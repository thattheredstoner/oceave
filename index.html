Covering over two-thirds of our blue planet, the ocean makes it habitable. It is at the origin of all life on Earth and affects each of our lives: it is the source of our freshwater and of half the oxygen we breathe; it also influences our climate and weather. Our ocean provides food, medicine, and mineral and energy resources. It supports a multitude of life forms and shapes the Earthâ€™s characteristics. Millions of tons of plastic enter the oceans each year, the majority of which spills out of rivers. A part of this plastic ends up in ocean garbage patches, where it becomes entangled in a vortex of cycling currents. If nothing is done, plastic will have a greater impact on our environment, health, and economies.
